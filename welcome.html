Rules

  1. Everyone is invited to make suggestions: mums, dads, grannies, grandpas, uncles and aunts, brothers and sisters (big and little), friends, relatives in Australia – and you.

  2. You can make as many suggestions as you like, and reply to this survey as many times as you like (every time another great book occurs to you, send another response.

  3. You can write as much or as little as you like. Just leave the name of the book or – particularly if the book is not well known (in this country at least) – say a little bit about it. Why must it be included in the 100 books?
